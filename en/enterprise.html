Hi. How can we help?

Where is the dashboard?

The dashboard is accessible in your internet browser with a username and password.

Can I revoke access to documents for a recipient?

Yes, you can revoke all or part of the emails and documents of a chosen recipient. It can be useful when one of your employees leaves or get their computer stolen.

Is it possible to track the opening of an email or a document?

Yes, audit trails make it possible to track the opening of emails and documents more precisely than a simple reading acknowledgment.

Is there access to documents and emails on the dashboard?

No, the dashboard only provides you with the tracking information of the documents, you will not be able to have access to the emails or documents. Seald does not have access to your documents because they do not transit on our servers.
